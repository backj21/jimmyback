My First Hackathon

Mar 2026 4 min read Jimmy Back

HackIllinois was my first full hackathon experience, and it completely changed how I think about building. I worked with my team on ease 'n spect, a mobile AI-assisted inspection workflow for heavy machinery.

The biggest lesson was speed with clarity. We had to make decisions quickly, keep the product flow simple, and avoid overbuilding features that did not improve the core user experience.

We focused on a clean end-to-end story: guided capture, voice notes, analysis, and a final summary that operators could actually use. That focus helped us ship something cohesive under real time pressure.

Presenting the final product was just as important as writing the code. Telling a clear technical and product story is what helped us stand out and place first in the Caterpillar track.
